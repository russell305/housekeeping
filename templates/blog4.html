How to clean your computer.

6/28/2019

Germs and bacteria often lurk in a variety of home office equipment. Keyboards, mouse, monitors and smart phones are easily contaminated by hand contact. This can pose health risks for you and your family, making them sick and possibly transferring germs to other family members. Sanitize your office equipment to prevent the spread of bacteria and disease. The process doesn’t require a professional cleaning crew and can be done using a few household supplies.

  • Turn the power off to the computer.
  • Start by giving your keyboard a good clean. Unplug your keyboard from your computer, or turn it if off if it's wireless. 
  • If you have a laptop, turn it off and unplug the charging cord. If you can easily remove the battery, it wouldn't hurt to do that too.
