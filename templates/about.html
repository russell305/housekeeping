House Cleaning Miami

I created this platform because I was unhappy with the options presented to house cleaners and consumers. Other platforms are over-engineered and purely foucused on extracting as much money as possible from their users. As opposed to solving their user's issues.

House Cleaners: Platforms extract a percentage of every dollar a house cleaner earns, or they will charge a $20 fee if somebody emails the house cleaner to ask a queston. They call this a "Lead". These practices unfair to the people doing the actual work. Here I offer a platform where house cleaners can offer their services for reasonable price.

Consumers: Most platforms require users to insert personal data subjet to hackers. Name, address, email, phone, bank information, etc. Others make you join a subscription service where you have your house cleaned twice a month for a year. Here I want to simplify the process where you can compare house cleaning services, and call and talk to the ones you like.
