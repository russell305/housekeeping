5 Quick Bathroom Cleaning Tips

9/20/2019

Cleaning the bathroom can cause anxiety and quickly become laborious. Follow these handy 5 tips daily to lessen the amount of bathroom cleaning that needs to be done….

  1. Sink - Use baby wipes daily! Keep a box under the sink, and use to wipe down the sinks and toilet after your morning use. When your, done just toss in the trash.
  2. Bathroom mirror - Use warm water and a damp rag. Try to use a rag not washed with a fabric softener. Fabric softeners will leave streaks. 
  3. Shower - While showering use a damp rag and some shampoo to wipe the walls of your shower. The will keep your shower clean and smelling fresh for longer periods before a deep cleaning is necessary. Don’t forget to wipe the shower head, handle, hose and shower knobs.
  4. Toilet - Did you know? Denture tablets like Polident can give a nice effervescent shine to the toilet bowl. Simply drop one in and wait a few minutes for the table to work its magic!
  5. Shower Curtain - When they start getting dirty and grimy, it can be difficult to just wipe it off. Instead just toss in the washing machine and wash on regular cycle.

Thank you for reading, hopefully using some of these tips will keep your house cleaner longer! Saving money by not needing a house cleaner as soon as you normally would.
